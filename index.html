Cakebot

I built a CNC platform and software for frosting cakes! This was done during the Principles of Engineering course at Olin College in 2015. The embedded control software ran on an Arduino. I created a user GUI in Python that sent control signals to the Arduino over a serial connection. Users could choose from several preset patterns, or draw their own.

Google Play music import/export tool

I wanted to transfer my library to a new account, and also potentially to a Spotify account. However, there was no good way to get plaintext data about my library, and use that data to build a new library with a new account. This small Python project lets you export your library and playlist information to JSON, and reimport it into a library. Could potentially be used in the future to transfer libraries between music services. Check it out on Github here.
